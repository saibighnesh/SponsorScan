SponsorScan

Stop guessing and start applying! SponsorScan instantly scans job postings across LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and other major job boards to tell you if a company is willing to sponsor your work visa.

Whether you're looking for an H-1B in the US, a Skilled Worker Visa in the UK, a Blue Card in Germany, or an LMIA in Canada, this tool highlights the exact phrases that indicate whether a job is open to international candidates or restricted to local citizens.

🚀 Key Features:
• Instant Detection: Click the extension icon on any job posting to immediately see its visa sponsorship status (Positive, Negative, or Unclear).
• Highlight on Page: With one click, highlight the exact phrases in the job description (Green for positive, Red for negative, Yellow for ambiguous) so you can read the context yourself.
• Multi-Language Support: Detects phrases in English, German, French, and Dutch.
• Smart Contextual Analysis: Uses an advanced engine to prevent false positives (e.g., understanding that "no visa sponsorship" is a negative signal, not a positive one).
• Global Country Detection: Automatically detects which country the job is located in based on currencies, cities, and local visa names (e.g., £, €, Tier 2, TSS).
