# Privacy Policy for SponsorScan

**Last updated: March 3, 2026**

SponsorScan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
SponsorScan is designed to operate entirely locally on your device. We **do not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or job search data.

When you click the extension icon:
- The extension briefly accesses the text content (`document.body.innerText`) and URL of the currently active tab.
- This text is analyzed locally within your browser using our built-in phrase dictionary to detect visa sponsorship signals.

## 2. How We Use Information
The text content retrieved from the active tab is used **strictly for real-time analysis** to provide you with the visa sponsorship status of the job posting you are currently viewing.

- We do not save this text.
- We do not send this text to any external servers or third-party APIs.
- The data exists only in your browser's memory for the duration of the analysis.

## 3. Permissions Explained
To function correctly, the extension requires the following permissions:
- **`activeTab`**: Allows the extension to read the text of the job posting you are currently looking at, only when you explicitly click the extension icon.
- **`scripting`**: Allows the extension to inject the logic required to read the text and apply visual highlights to the job description on the page.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services within the extension.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
